1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owners. They help us recognize your browser and remember your preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality and security (e.g., login sessions).
  • Performance Cookies: To analyze site usage and improve our features.
  • Functional Cookies: To remember your settings like theme preference (dark/light mode).
  • Advertising Cookies: To deliver relevant advertisements through third-party services like Google AdSense.

3. Managing Your Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your preferences through your browser settings or by using our cookie settings manager.
