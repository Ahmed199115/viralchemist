Privacy Policy

At ViralChemist , your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our AI tools.

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

In Plain English

  • We collect minimal data needed to make our service work (like your email if you sign up).
  • The content you generate is yours. We don't store it or use it.
  • We use analytics to improve our tools, but this data is anonymized.
  • We will never sell your personal data. Period.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Your name and email address if you create an account or contact us.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized analytics (e.g., which tools are used most) to improve our service.
  • Cookies: Small files to remember your preferences (like dark mode). See our Cookies Policy .

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and improve our AI tools.
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Send important service updates (you can opt-out of marketing emails).

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Access is strictly limited to authorized personnel.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. To do so, please contact us at privacy@viralchemist.com .

5. Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available on this page.
